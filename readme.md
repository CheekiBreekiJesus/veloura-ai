Overview

Veloura AI is an advanced AI-powered styling and beauty companion designed to provide users with a highly personalized fashion and appearance experience.

The platform combines:

AI face and body analysis

Outfit recommendations

Personal color analysis

Hairstyle and makeup suggestions

Virtual wardrobe management

Product matching and affiliate commerce

AI conversational assistance through Aura

Future virtual mannequin and try-on systems


Veloura is designed to feel less like a utility app and more like a personal stylist companion that users interact with daily.

The long-term vision is to create a complete AI identity and styling ecosystem that understands:

Facial structure

Personal aesthetic

Clothing preferences

Body proportions

Seasonal color palettes

Lifestyle

Fashion goals

Confidence and self-expression

yarn install
